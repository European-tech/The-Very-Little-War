all alternatives. Strategic diversity is illusory.

================================================================================
CRITICAL FINDINGS (REQUIRE ACTION)
================================================================================

1. P1-D4-020: DOMINANT ARMY COMPOSITION [HIGH SEVERITY]
   Issue: One composition always wins (Class 1: Br/C tank, Class 2-4: O damage)
   Impact: No strategy diversity, game solved
   Fix: Add counter-mechanics (special formations, molecule diversity bonuses)

2. P1-D4-027: ATTACK > DEFENSE ASYMMETRY [MEDIUM SEVERITY]
   Issue: Attacker EV (+325) is 2.6x higher than defender EV (+125)
   Impact: Everyone attacks, no one defends, aggressive play always wins
   Fix: Increase defense reward from 20% to 30% of pillage capacity

3. P1-D4-029: BEGINNER PROTECTION TOO SHORT [MEDIUM-HIGH SEVERITY]
   Issue: 3-day protection insufficient, new players unprotected 92% of season
   Impact: New players can't catch up, immediate victim pool for established players
   Fix: Extend to 5-7 days AND add 2x production boost for first week

4. P1-D4-031: SOUFRE PILLAGE OVERLY PROFITABLE [MEDIUM SEVERITY]
   Issue: Single attack can yield 20 months of victim production
   Impact: Exponential wealth concentration, early winners dominate forever
   Fix: Add 10% pillage tax OR defensive building OR formula rebalance

================================================================================
FORMATION BALANCE ISSUES
================================================================================

5. P1-D4-021: PHALANGE OVERPOWERED [MEDIUM SEVERITY]
   - Class 1 absorbs 60% + gets +20% bonus (unfair vs. Dispersée/Embuscade)
   - Recommendation: Reduce absorb from 60% to 50%

6. P1-D4-022: EMBUSCADE UNDERWHELMING [MEDIUM SEVERITY]
   - Only +25% bonus, only when outnumbering, defender-only advantage
   - Recommendation: Increase bonus to +40% OR add secondary effect

================================================================================
ISOTOPE & ATOM BALANCE ISSUES
================================================================================

7. P1-D4-023: RÉACTIF OFTEN SUPERIOR [MEDIUM SEVERITY]
   - Decay irrelevant in 30-day season, so high attack >> tank HP
   - Recommendation: Buff Stable (increase HP bonus from +30% to +40%)

8. P1-D4-032: CHLORE SPEED RARELY IMPACTFUL [LOW SEVERITY]
   - 31x speed bonus but travel times are minutes vs. hours cooldown
   - Recommendation: Make speed affect cooldown (reduce by 5% per 20 Cl)

9. P1-D4-030: VAULT PROTECTION INSUFFICIENT [MEDIUM SEVERITY]
   - Max 50% protection but costs 160k+ energy (3-4x monthly production)
   - Recommendation: Reduce cost scaling OR increase protection rate
